# Monofacture — Economy & Smart Contracts Design

## Fee Model
- Channel price P set by owner (e.g. 10 TON)
- Platform commission: 5% of P
- Advertiser pays: P * 1.05 → smart contract
- On success: contract → platform wallet → backend distributes
- Publisher gets P in app balance, platform keeps 5%
- If referrer: 20% of commission → referrer balance

## Referral: 20% of platform fee (1% of deal)
## Withdrawal: min 1 TON, ~0.05 TON network fee
